4 What if something changed?

Everything here is applied as an hourly shape, not a monthly total. Charging an EV at 1am and at 6pm cost very different amounts for identical kWh, and the same is true in reverse for a solar panel.

What this does and doesn't know

Rates are extracted from filed SDG&E tariff sheets and published CCA rate schedules, and the engine is checked against a real bill to within 0.1%. It is still an estimate — not a quote, and not affiliated with SDG&E, San Diego Community Power, or Clean Energy Alliance.
